# Welcome to Blackridge County

## Rules & Roleplay Guidelines Overview

Welcome to Blackridge County, a serious roleplay environment built around immersive storytelling, meaningful consequences, and fair play. These rules and guidelines exist to ensure every player can participate in high-quality roleplay while maintaining a respectful and balanced community.

All players are expected to read, understand, and follow the rules outlined across the sections below. Ignorance of the rules is not an excuse for violations. Staff reserve the right to interpret and enforce these rules in the interest of roleplay quality and server health.

## Final Notes

Blackridge County prioritizes story over winning, roleplay over mechanics, and respect over ego. Players are expected to accept consequences, communicate in good faith, and contribute to the shared world.

If you are ever unsure about a rule, ask staff before acting. When issues arise, complete the roleplay first (unless it is severely harmful), then use the proper reporting channels.
